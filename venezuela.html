Facts about Venezuela and its flag

The flag of Venezuela is one that is rather boring and simple, but it is the best flag in South America (in my opinion). It is a horizontal tricolor made up of yellow, blue and red. The flag was without the stars from 1811 to 2006.

The colours have great meaning. The yellow represents the riches of the country, as well as the Sun. Blue represents the Sea of the Caribbean that surrounds the country, as well as their beaches, and the read represents the blood that was spilt in the battle of independence from Spain
