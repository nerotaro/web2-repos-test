Facts about the Marshall Islands and its flag

The flag of Marshall Islands has a blue background, with orange and white coming from the bottom-left corner and exiting on the top of the right side. The flag also features a 24 poined star.

Marshall Islands has changed flag multiple times throughout its history. It had a really cool flag during it's German colonial rule (1878-1894) and was also used by the UN as a trust territory. It was for the most of its lifetime a colony of the United States.
