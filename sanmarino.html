Facts about San Marino and its flag

The flag of San Marino is a bicolor consisting of two horizontal bands, with a light blue on the bottom and white on the top. Light blue represents liberty, and white represents peace. The coat of arms of San Marino is in the middle of the flag, with a scroll under it saying "LIBERTAS" which means Liberty.

The state flag of San Marino is also used as their war flag, even though they have only been around in WW2, and was involved in one battle (The Battle of San Marino).
