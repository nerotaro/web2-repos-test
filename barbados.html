Facts about Barbados and its flag

The flag of Barbados is a rather simple, but great looking flag. It consists of a triband of two blue bands, seperated in the middle by a gold band, and a black trident head in the center.

The blue on the flag is an ultramarine blue, and it represents the ocean and sky surronding Barbados. The gold colour represents the sand, and the trident head is often reffered to as "the Broken Trident".
