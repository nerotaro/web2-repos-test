Facts about the Seychelles and its flag

The flag of the Seychelles is a very unique flag, because the five bands that it consists of start from the bottom-left corner of the flag, and they diverge outwards.

The five coloured bands are blue, representing the sky and the sea that surrounds the Seychelles, yellow, representing the sun, red, representing the people, white, representing social justice and harmony and green, representing the land and natural environment.

The Seychelles is also quite unique in that it never had any indigenous population, and was colonized by the French and British.
