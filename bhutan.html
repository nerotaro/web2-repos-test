Facts about Bhutan and its flag

Bhutan has a very unusual flag because of its Chinese dragon. It actually features the dragon to allude to the name of Bhutan in the Dzongkha language, where Bhutan roughly translates to "Dragon Country" or "Dragon Kingdom".

The yellow on the flag is there to represent a yellow scarf that the Dragon King of Bhutan wears. The orange on the other hand, represents the Buddhist spiritual tradition of the country. It is divided like this to show the equal importance of the both civic and monastic traditions in Bhutan
